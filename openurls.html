About

OpenURLs is a powerful tool designed to open multiple URLs simultaneously. It's perfect for those who need to access multiple websites quickly, such as web-hosting providers, digital marketing executives, and anyone who frequently checks multiple links at once. With OpenURLs, you can open 20-25 URLs at a time with just a single click, saving you time and effort.

How to Use

  1. Copy (CTRL+C) and paste (CTRL+V) your URLs into the text area above. Each URL should be on a separate line.
  2. Set the delay between opening links and choose whether to open in new tabs or windows.
  3. Click the "Open" button to start opening the web pages instantly in your browser.

Where You Can Use It

OpenURLs is a one-click, no-install required tool that works on any updated web browser. It's compatible with all editions of Windows, Linux, and iOS operating systems, and can be accessed on computers, smartphones, and tablets.

FAQ

Is there a limit to how many URLs I can open?

There's no strict limit, but opening too many URLs at once may cause your browser to freeze momentarily. We recommend opening 20-25 URLs at a time for optimal performance.

Is it safe to use OpenURLs?

Yes, OpenURLs is completely safe to use. It's a client-side tool that runs in your browser and doesn't store or transmit any of your data.

Can I use OpenURLs on my mobile device?

Yes, OpenURLs is designed to be responsive and works on mobile devices. However, the experience may vary depending on your device and browser settings.

How to Allow Your Browser to Open Multiple Links

1. After clicking the "Open" button, you may see a prompt at the top of your browser.

2. Click on the "Allow" or "Always allow" option in the prompt.

3. If you don't see a prompt, check for a pop-up blocker icon on the right side of your browser's address bar.

4. Click on this icon and select "Always allow pop-ups from this site".
